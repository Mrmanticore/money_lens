ABOUT US

Money Lens is a currency recognition application designed to accurately identify Indian currency notes in real-time. This tool leverages advanced machine learning models and computer vision techniques to recognize various denominations, including ₹10, ₹20, ₹50, ₹100, ₹200, ₹500, and ₹2000 notes. Using a live camera feed, Money Lens captures images of currency notes, predicts the denomination, and provides a confidence score to verify the prediction accuracy. Additionally, an integrated audio feature announces the denomination for enhanced accessibility and ease of use.

This project is built using Flask for the backend, OpenCV for real-time video processing, and Roboflow’s API for leveraging machine learning models trained on thousands of Indian currency images. Money Lens is a highly interactive tool, designed for seamless performance across different devices, making it ideal for those needing rapid and reliable currency recognition, such as visually impaired users, cashiers, or anyone dealing with large amounts of cash daily.
